These are notes on how to formulate different scenarios

Invisible Hand:
    Here are the choices/payoffs for when the dominant strategy is bottom right. 
    Bad,Good,Bad,Good,2,1,3,4,2,3,1,4

    For when it is in the top left.
    Good,Bad,Good,Bad,4,3,1,2,4,1,3,2

    Here are the choices/payoffs for when the dominant strategy is bottom right. 
    High Prices, Low Prices, High Prices, Low Prices, 3,1,4,2,3,4,1,2

    For when it is top left.
    Low Prices, High Prices, Low Prices, High Prices, 2,3,1,4,2,1,3,4
